My Story

Hi, I'm Lucia! It would seem almost superfluous to specify that I love the blue skies and the sea of ​​my Sicily, but I feel them so much mine that I cannot omit it.

I am a curious person who is constantly looking for as many small details as possible. When I was a child and I received a new toy as a gift, I first disassembled it to figure out what was "behind it" that allowed it to function and look good, and then I enjoyed putting all the pieces back together.

One of my greatest passions is computer science: everything that is "behind" what we see on the screen has always fascinated me!

I like to think and always solve new problems, I like challenges, and that's why I decided to devote myself to Web development!
